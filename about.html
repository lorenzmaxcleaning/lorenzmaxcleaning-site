About Lorenz Max Cleaning

Professional, reliable cleaning for busy families and businesses.

Lorenz Max Cleaning LLC is a locally owned and operated company based in Lancaster County, Pennsylvania. We specialize in residential and commercial cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in/move-out services, carpet care, and hotel housekeeping support.

As an Executive Housekeeper with years of experience in the hospitality industry, our founder brings hotel-level standards of cleanliness, organization, and attention to detail into every home and business we service.

Our mission

Our mission is simple: to provide reliable, consistent, and high-quality cleaning so you can enjoy more free time and a healthier, more comfortable space. We believe great cleaning is not only about how it looks, but also about how it feels to live and work there.
